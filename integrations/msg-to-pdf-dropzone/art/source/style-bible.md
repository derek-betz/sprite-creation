# 2.5D Theater Style Bible

## Target

Original sci-fantasy command workshop with game-grade readability.

## Shape Language

- Heavy armored courier silhouette with broad shoulders and a narrow waist
- Tall archways, angled buttresses, and floating brass trim
- Stations read like mini dioramas, not icons

## Palette

- Base world: charcoal, obsidian, oil-blue steel
- Warm accents: brass, amber, furnace orange
- Cool magic: azure, teal, glacial white
- Delivery success: luminous mint
- Failure: ember red

## Lighting

- Strong top-down moon-forge lighting
- Local station glows push colored light into nearby surfaces
- Floor reflections stay subtle and broken, not glossy

## Materials

- Brushed metal
- Stone inlaid with brass
- Crystal glass and rune lenses
- Fabric banners and cloak trim

## Production Rule

Any AI-generated concept must be cleaned before export:
- fix perspective drift
- sharpen silhouettes
- repair hands and armor seams
- remove warped text and fake interface marks
- align lighting across all plates
